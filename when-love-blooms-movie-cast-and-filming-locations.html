Where Was When Love Blooms Filmed?

When Love Blooms was filmed in Montreal, Quebec, Canada.

Incendo Productions started shooting the movie in the summer of May 2021 in Montreal.

Montreal is a popular filming location for movies and television shows because of its architecture and culture.

The sites used for shooting are Old Montreal, Bonsecours Market, Notre-Dame Basilica, and McGill University campuses.

In the movie, Ann Keller and Aaron Blum’s first encounter in Dales Market, and the florist suggest her mac n cheese is something special here.
