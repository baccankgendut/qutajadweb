Career

In 1982, he started his career with Ted Lipscomb. Arn spends his time in several independent wrestling companies in the United States. He wrestled one year for Mid South Wrestling in 1982 and 1983. The producer left the company and joined Mid South Wrestling, which based in Shreveport. Arn created his way for Jim Crockett, Jr’s Mid-Atlantic Championship Wrestling, which based in Virginias and Carolinas. Soon Anderson received legendary status in Georgia and Mid-Atlantic territories.  In 1985, the wrestle won NWA National Tag Team Championship. They successfully defended their title against Wahoo McDaniel and Billy Jack Haynes.

In 1985, the ARN allied with Ric Flair and Tully Blanchard. The group often involved in six-man matches, eight-man tag matches, and many more. On September 10, 1988, they lost NWA World Tag Team Championship to Midnight Express.

Arn Anderson left NWA and joined WWF. He was named The Brain Buster in the promotion. The tag team of Arn became very popular. They challenged Demolition for the WWF Tag Team Championship and won the titles.

In 1993, the wrestle joined Ole and Ric to found Four Horsemen. The group added new member Paul Roma. In August, he and Roma won WCW World Tag Team Championship. He remained with WCW for a few years.
