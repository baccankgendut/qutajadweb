Early Life & Biography

Heather Thomas, whose full name is Heather Anne Thomas, was born on the 8th of September 1957 in Greenwich, Connecticut, the U.S. to parents, Gladdy Lou Ryder, and Leon Thomas. Her mother worked as a special education teacher in the Santa Monica-Malibu Unified School District. She has attended the Santa Monica High School, from where she graduated in 1975 and UCLA School of Theater, Film, and Television, from where she graduated in 1980. She was a member of the Chi Omega sorority at UCLA.
