Kendall Jenner and her new boujee alcohol, 818 tequila, are getting torn apart on social media as some feel it is offensive and "modern colonizer vibes."

Taking to social media to launch the brand, Kendall shared a snap from the campaign with great pride. Never did she think that her braided hair and horse would cause this much commotion.

“What an incredible experience I have had thus far, learning about this beautiful place, it’s beautiful culture, and the beautiful people! @drink818 has launched in California... we will be rolling out to the rest of the US all summer long, keep a look out!!!”

Kendall turned off the comments to the post as she was getting a lot of negative backlash. The people took to Twitter to share their frustrations.

“No hate, but no matter what happen with Kendal Jenner, I think the people get power to not buy that “tequila” ! Just say it. ♀️”“Kendal Jenner is such a colonizer. I literally can’t”“Not Kendal Jenner exploiting our culture to make money. Her commercial fed into so many stereotypes, goodbye.”
