Career

Kim teamed up with her sister Kyle Richardson in the 1977 thriller film The Car. She as well appeared in the movie Devil Dog: The Hound of Hell, released in 1978. During this time, she also made appearances as a guest in various television shows, including Emergency, The Love Boat, The Dukes of Hazzard, Little House on the Prairie, and Magnum P.I., among others.
