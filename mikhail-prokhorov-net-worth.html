What is Mikhail Prokhorov’s Net Worth?

Mikhail Prokhorov, a Russian oligarch and politician, has an estimated net worth of $13.5 billion. He made his fortune by “acquiring” the formerly state-owned Norilsk Nickel and turning it into a massive global energy conglomerate. Prokhorov is also the former chairman of Polyus Gold, the largest gold producer in Russia.

Early Life

Mikhail Prokhorov was born on May 3, 1965, in Moscow, Soviet Union. He comes from a Jewish descent and grew up with a father who was involved in international relations for the Soviet Committee of Physical Culture and Sport. Prokhorov attended Moscow Finance Institute and graduated in 1989.
