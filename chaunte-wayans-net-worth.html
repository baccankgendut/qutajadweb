Chaunté’s entry into the entertainment world was marked by her role as a production assistant.

Who is Chaunte Wayans?

Chaunté Wayans is a multi-talented American comedian, actress, and writer. She is known for her unique comedic style and being part of the famous Wayans family dynasty in Hollywood.

What is she known for in her career?

She has appeared in several TV shows and movies, including Wild ‘N Out on MTV, Trading Spaces on TLC, and films like Dance Flick and Hollywood Misconceptions. She is also recognized for her stand-up comedy performances across the country.

She began her career working as a production assistant on films like Scary Movie 2 and on the TV show My Wife and Kids before making her way into acting and stand-up comedy.

Is she part of the Wayans family?

Yes, she is a member of the renowned Wayans family. She is the niece of Keenen Ivory Wayans, Damon Wayans, Sr., Kim Wayans, Shawn Wayans, and Marlon Wayans, and her brother is Damien Dante Wayans.

What are some personal challenges she has discussed?

She has openly shared her experiences with personal identity, discussing her life as a lesbian and how it has influenced her comedy. She has also talked about her battles with substance abuse, using her platform to advocate for sobriety and mental health.

Aside from her entertainment career, she is active in community service. She spends time volunteering by feeding the homeless, performing at women’s shelters, and giving talks on bullying at schools.
