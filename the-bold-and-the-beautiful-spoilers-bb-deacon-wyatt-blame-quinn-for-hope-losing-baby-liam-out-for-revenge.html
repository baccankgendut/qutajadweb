The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers for the episode airing on Wednesday, December 10 tease another emotional and dramatic day on the CBS soap. Everyone is still buzzing about Hope Logan’s unfortunate accident and miscarriage – and Quinn Fuller appears to be at the top of everyone’s hit list. Ironically, for the first time ever, Quinn actually wasn’t responsible for Hope’s fall – but considering her track record, the Bold and the Beautiful spoiler video for tomorrow’s episode reveals that no one is buying it.

Bold and the Beautiful spoilers for Wednesday December 10 reveal that Wyatt will spend the episode at Hope’s bed side in the hospital. In the all new B&B spoiler video for tomorrow a distraught Wyatt questions Hope about her fall and urges her, “If my mother had anything to do with this, you have to tell me. Okay?” Wyatt Fuller isn’t the only man in Quinn’s life that is blaming her for Hope’s miscarriage. The Bold and the Beautiful promo video for December 10th also features Deacon Sharpe crying and screaming at Quinn, “That’s all you had to do, just leave it alone!”

Meanwhile, Bold and the Beautiful spoilers tease that Liam (who ironically isn’t Hope’s husband nor was he the baby’s father) is the most upset over Hope’s accident and appears to be out for revenge. In the B&B spoiler video for tomorrow’s episode of the CBS soap, Liam tells Ivy, “Quinn was there and something bad happened and I think that makes her responsible.”

So B&B fans, do you agree with Deacon and Liam? Was the accident Quinn’s fault because she upset Hope and stressed her out before she walked down the stairs? Or for once was Quinn innocent and Hope’s loved ones are just looking for someone to blame? Will Liam come after Quinn and try to make her pay for Hope losing the baby?
