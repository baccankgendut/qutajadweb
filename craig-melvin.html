Birth Details and Academics

Being born to Melvin's family, Craig holds American nationality and belongs to an African-American ethnic group. Craig was born on 20th, May 1979 in Columbia, South Carolina, the United States of America to Lawrence Melvin (father), and Betty Melvin (mother). 

His parents raised Craig Melvin in Columbia along with his two siblings; Rev.Lawrence Melvin ( older brother), and Ryan Melvin (younger brother). Craig was born under the zodiac sign Taurus. 

Further, he attended Columbia High School and earned his Bachelor of Arts Degree from Wofford College. 

Professional Career

Craig Melvin used to work as a teen reporter for WIS News 10. He joined the local news channel, WIS News 10 just after graduating from college in 2001. 

Whereas, he also worked as a weekday anchor, a feature reporter, and a weekend news anchor for many NEWS channels. Later in 2011, he joined MSNBC as a daytime anchor. He also worked as a co-host for MSNBC Live Weekend. 

Recently, Craig is a part of NBC news. He started working as a news anchor for NBC News in 2012 and later became an anchor there. Similarly, his most noticeable show for NBC news was 'Weekend Today' where Craig was co-anchor till 2018. 

Throughout his journalism career, he interviewed many famous personalities such as; Nicki Minaj, Feidin Santana, and Bill Clinton. Similarly, he has worked with a journalist like Kasie Hunt, Andrea Mitchell, Katy Tur, Lester Holt, and many more.
