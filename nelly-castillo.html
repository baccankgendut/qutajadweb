Nelly Castillo, an American actress who was born on July 2, 1980, in the tropical port of Veracruz, Mexico, where she received a Bachelor of Arts in Communications from the Universidad Autonoma Villa Rica, an affiliate of the prestigious Universidad Nacional Autonoma de Mexico (UNAM).

After being crowned Miss “Nuestra Belleza Veracruz Boca del Rio‘, Nelly was allowed to co-host “Soy Tiburon Superior” a Television produced sports show directed at soccer fans.
