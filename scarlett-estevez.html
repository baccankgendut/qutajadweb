Age, Height, And Weight

Scarlett Estevez aka Scar (nickname) was born on 4 December 2007 to father Anthony Estevez and mother Samantha Weil Estevez in Los Angeles, California, the United States. She grew up in her hometown with his parents and siblings: older sister Eloise Estevez, and younger brother Ben Estevez. 

Scarlett Estevez is 13 years old and holds an American nationality. She belongs to a mixed ethnic background. Estevez is 4 feet 9 inches (145 cm) and weighs around 46 kg (102 pounds). 

Career Highlights

Scarlett Estevez debuted in the entertainment industry as a child actress. She did not begin as an actor, but rather as a business model. Estevez landed her first nationwide commercial when she was just three years old.

Following her first infomercials, Scarlett appeared in several commercials. Later in 2013, she got a role in the TV series Redeeming Dave. A year later, she appeared as Young Ashley in the short movie The Magic Bracelet. 

Then, Scar made an appearance in movies and TV series like And Then There Was You, The Massively Mixed-Up Middle School Mystery, Modest Heroes, and The Grinch, to name a few. In 2015, 13 years old actress played as Megan in the 2015 American comedy film Daddy’s Home. With her performance, she reprised the role of Megan in the sequel of Daddy’s Home. 

Finally, in 2016, she got her breakthrough in the film industry through the recurring role of Trixie Espinoza in an American urban fantasy TV series Lucifer. The TV series starred Tom Ellis, Lauren German, Kevin Alejandro, and other co-stars. She appeared in a total of 72 episodes. 

In 2019, Estevez appeared in the American comedy TV series Bunk’d as Gwen Flores alongside Peyton List, Karan Brar, Miranda May, Nina Lu, and others. 

Regarding her future projects, Scarlett Estevez is set to be appearing in 2021 and onwards releasing the movie Christmas Again. 

Awards And Accolades
