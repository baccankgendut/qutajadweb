Fancy prams: 5 celebrities who absolutely splurged on their strollers

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Celebrities are used to travelling in style, so it is only fitting that the splendour extends to their babies' modes of transport. Here are 5 celebs who decided a pricey pram was the only way to go.

PAY ATTENTION: Click “See First” under the “Following” tab to see We News on your News Feed!

It's no secret that celebrities love spending their cash and using it to enjoy some of the finer things in life, and the same holds true for when they're treating their kiddies.

The latest must-have accessory for new parents is not just a stroller that is functional, but one that is fashionable.

We looks at 5 celebrity parents who splashed out on prams.

5 Kenny Kunene

Kenny reportedly spent R80 000 back in 2017 for his little Romeo. As though it wasn't enough that the pram cost as much as a car, it went to an actual car wash for a clean.

4 Kylie Jenner

The youngest member of the Kardashian-Jenner clan is also the richest. Kylie splashed out on a Fendi stroller for her little Stormi which retails at around R150 000.

3 Ntando Mtethwa-Kunene

Ntando is a doting mommy which is why she spent a reported R11 000 on the stroller for her little bundle of joy.

2 Beyoncé and Jay-Z

The power couple welcomed twins, Rumi and Sir, in 2017. Beyoncé and Jay-Z got them matching Versace strollers for R56 000.

1 Cassper Nyovest

The newest member of the parental bunch is Mzansi's own Cassper Nyovest. Cass dropped around R32 000 on the stroller which is a Cybex Priam collab with Moschino designer Jeremy Scott.

We reported on Cass splashing out for the stroller when he first announced it on Twitter.

Cassper Nyovest is proving that he is bringing his own style and flair to his newly acquired dad-life. The rapper took to Twitter to post about the pram he recently purchased for little Khotso. Cassper showed off the stroller, which is a Cybex Priam x Jeremy Scott creation, and commented on how cool the contraption is.

The pram is all black with gold wings atop the little carriage. Jeremy Scott is the creative director of the fashion house Moschino and his collaboration on the stroller clearly adds to the price tag.

The pram retails from R32 000 here in South Africa, so the Any Minute Now hitmaker spared no expense when it came to his little guy's wheels.

Cassper captioned the photo:

"My pride and Joy. Lil man ridding around in a pram designed by @ITSJEREMYSCOTT. How dope ?"
